Build with Xcode

Full native app · Requires a Mac

The Xcode project is ready. Download ERP_Evaluator_iOS_XcodeProject.zip from the repo, open on a Mac with Xcode, and run directly to your iPhone via USB.
1
Download the Xcode project Get ERP_Evaluator_iOS_XcodeProject.zip from the GitHub repo.
2
Install dependencies On your Mac, open Terminal in the ios/App folder and run: pod install
3
Open in Xcode & connect your iPhone Open App.xcworkspace, select your device, tap ▶ Run.
4
Trust the developer certificate On iPhone: Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → Trust your Apple ID.
Free Apple ID OK Mac required Xcode 15+ Re-sign every 7 days (free) or $99/yr
🔄

AltStore Sideload

Install IPA without App Store · No Mac needed after setup

1
Install AltStore on your iPhone Download AltStore from altstore.io using AltInstaller on a PC or Mac.
2
Build a signed IPA on a Mac Use the Xcode project to Archive → Export → Ad Hoc distribution. This produces a signed .ipa file.
3
Sideload via AltStore Open AltStore on your iPhone, tap +, select the .ipa file, install.
AltStore setup required Mac needed once (to sign) Refresh every 7 days (free tier)
Tip: The Add to Home Screen method (Option 1) is the fastest and needs no developer account, Mac, or extra tools. For most CFOs evaluating the app, this is all you need.
